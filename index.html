Ringo has been in the kitchen working his magic with pizza dough since before Chef Gordon Ramsay was in diapers. His mother, father, his 13 siblings, five dogs and he arrived in the United States in 1953 after leaving Italy for a better life. The family wasn't the only thing that left Italy that day. The long held family marinara and pizza crust recipes handed down by generations of pizza makers that spanned as far back in time as the days of Da Vinci. Armed with this recipe the family decided to open up their restaurant Ringo's Pizza Joint in Spokane just a few months after they arrived at the Port of the mighty Spokane Bay. :D No not Coeur D'Alene, but Spokane Bay! Arghh mateys! Mama always expressed quality over quantity and that is why even though there have been several attempts by big corporate outfits to turn Ringo's into a national chain, Mama has decline preferring to cater solely to Spokane and those lucky inhabitants therein. The restaurant is located in the heart of downtown Spokane facing the beautiful Spokane Bay. If you squint hard enough you can see Killer Whales off towards the sunset as you dine at dinner on our pier dining deck.
